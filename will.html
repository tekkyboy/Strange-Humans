William Mencurkentang

"We all need to make choices for ourselves at the end of the day. There's nobody else to be there for you except yourself."

  • Gender: Male
  • Eye Color: Brown
  • Hair Color: Black
  • Hair Length: Short, to Neck
  • Zodiac Birth Sign: Taurus
I met Will while I was pledging for Alpha Phi Omega. He was just another person to cross off the interview list at the time, but we actually had a rather enjoyable conversation with each other.
We became friends shortly after I did my interview with him. It was a natural gravitation between two rather goofy but serious individuals, both studying the same major. It also helped that we were both friends with Megan. Additionally, we became better friends by the sheer coincidences of running into each other at the library consistently at the same times.
What I like about Will is the fact that he's a genuine and honest person, along with the fact that we've shared many similar interests and life experiences (as of Fall 2018 anyway). But I do consider him to be a life long friend on the basis that I can't imagine him not being in my life in some way, shape or form. While I wouldn't say that we're super close friends, I think that there continues to be an ideal of strong mutual respect between the two of us, which is why our friendship is still strong. While I'm aware he's more loyal to other people who aren't me, I do believe that if I really need Will in my life he can and he, no pun intended, will be there for me. I also consider Will to be a life long friend because I trust him to do the right thing. To help other people that I can't help out.
  1. Will is a really trusting friend and considers himself close to Megan, Emily, and Michael.
  2. Will considers himself to be an introvert and prefers having quality over quantity friendships.
  3. Will would prefer to work in business management over software engineering.
  4. Will doesn't like to use social media that much and prefers to live life off the social network grid.
  5. Will's personal philosophy is to "go with the flow" of life and live for yourself first before anyone else.
