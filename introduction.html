The Introduction

The following narrative is based on realistic events. But also fictional events. The following narrative happens during the present day. But it will also happen during the past, and maybe the future. The following narrative will involve plenty of people that I personally know in real life. It will also involve plenty of people who don't exist in the real world - a work of fiction. Whatever happens in this narrative, know that this narrative is crafted from the genuine intent to explain my life's story within a single continuous effort without risking the personal lives of the many individuals whom I personally know.
