Sara Hoshi

"I'm bored."

"I don't know what I want to do."

"So let's do something exciting today!!!"

  • Gender: Female
  • Eye Color: Black
  • Hair Color: Black
  • Hair Length: Long, to chest
  • Zodiac Birth Sign: Taurus
I first met Sara while she was pledging for Alpha Phi Omega. She was a rather socially awkward girl and I took an instant liking towards her as I myself was once a socially awkward person. I was rather invested in her because she was really quiet yet also very eccentric at the same time. She gravitated towards me easily at first, because I was an easy person to talk to.
We became friends over the pandemic, somewhat kept in touch with each other during the pandemic. It wasn't until Christmas 2020 when we genuinely became friends when I asked her to help me buy Christmas presents for my sisters. And after that, we became closer friends as she would constantly ask me to buy her boba while she was at home or whenever she was bored.
I like Sara because she's totally and completely different compared to many people I know. She lives life on a far different system and spectrum compared to most people that I know. She's genuinely fun to be around because you don't know what you're going to do when you hangout with her. Being with Sara is definitely one big act of improv. Think fast and just live in the moment when you're spending time with her.
I consider Sara to be my life long friend because she's a really unique girl and I don't think I could be genuinely happy without her in my life to some degree or capacity. I do feel like I depend on Sara for a sense of personal stability.
