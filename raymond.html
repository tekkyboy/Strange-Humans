Raymond Maudo

"I'm probably not the best man for the job, but I'll do my best anyway!"

  • Gender: Male
  • Eye Color: Brown
  • Hair Color: Black
  • Hair Length: Short
  • Zodiac Birth Sign: Taurus
I first met Raymond while I was pledging for Alpha Phi Omega, just as another person to interview. We rarely talked to each other while I was pledging. We've maintained steady contact over the years. Most of the time, he reminded me on my limits as to what to say and what not to say, how to act and how not to act, and so forth.
We became friends much later after I was done with Alpha Phi Omega. We ran into each other on campus back in Spring 2022, and we took the time to casually talk about the state of affairs regarding the community around us. We talked and we connected over how much we agreed on many talking points about conflicts and events that happened in the past. It was this connection and determination to prevent pointless conflicts to happen again which cemented him as a good friend. Someone worth mentioning as one of the many strange, strange humans in this world.
What I like about Raymond is the fact that he is a caring individual, someone who makes strangers feel included in his life. He understands the values of hardships and struggles, so he'll do his best to help out everyone else and alleviate their burdens. Raymond is the type of guy you could trust to be on your side, especially when you feel like the rest of the world is conspiring against you. He also has a strong morale side, so you'll know that he'll always be on your side. I consider Raymond to be a life long friend because of his morality, even though we've just started being friends with each other. I like having people like him around to remind me about what the normal moral boundaries are for most people, given my alien perspective on human life. I also admire his long lasting relationship with his girlfriend, which is something I'd like to have one day.
