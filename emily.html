Emily Wyns

"I'm always bringing my A game everywhere I go."

  • Gender: Female
  • Eye Color: Brown
  • Hair Color: Orange Red
  • Hair Length: To Shoulders
  • Zodiac Birth Sign: Taurus
I met Emily Wyns back while I was pledging for Alpha Phi Omega. Emily Wyns was also involved with the fraternity but had retired from active life by the time I met her. We met each other somewhere on the fourth floor of the college library, where she happened to be catching up with other members from the fraternity. I was able to talk to her for some time, thinking that she was part of our interview checklist. She was not. I still talked to her regardless as I would have wanted to talk to anybody anyway.
I became friends with Emily Wyns slowly over time as I ran into her on campus constantly at random places. Mostly, we would talk and share our pledging experiences. We also became friends because we've had mutual friendships through Will and Megan. We also kept in touch over the pandemic with occasional words of encouragement, commenting on the odd photo on our social media platforms here and there. I also invited her to my 21st birthday party and was genuinely surprised to see that she showed up. It was the fact that she showed up, even though we've barely made a meaningful friendship over the past three years, was what proved to me that we had a genuine friendship.
What I like about Emily Wyns is the fact that she's relatively down to Earth and very nice to talk to. While I don't have an overtly deep and meaningful friendship with her, we do hold a great amount of respect for one another. Emily was also one of the few people who treated everyone equally and fairly. While she was closer to Megan compared to most people, it was a special type of non-blood sibling relationship between the two of them. I think the fact that she was open to talk to me, even though I was a rather socially awkward person at the time, was what made me so impressed by her and her character. She didn't overtly care about how other people percieved her, the fact that she would take the time to know some oddball like myself.
I consider Emily to be a life long friend on the sole purpose that I like hanging out with her. I like maintaining a nice friendship with her, even if it won't become overtly deep and complicated. Someonetimes, nice friendships are all one needs with another person. Nothing too complicated or complex is necessary to enjoy another person's company. I think also the fact that she's still talking to me even though she doesn't have any value to continue talking to me is a nice benefit on its own.
