Caroline Moore

"Everything is interesting to me because I don't understand it yet."

  • Gender: Female
  • Eye Color: Dark Blue
  • Hair Color: Blonde
  • Hair Length: Up to neck
  • Zodiac Birth Sign: Taurus
I first met Caroline Moore back in 2005. We attended the same elementary school together. But we never really talked to each other during that time of our lives. I've started talking to her somewhat in middle school during 2012-2014, but it was rather superficial talk at the time. Then in high school, we had a couple of classes for the first year but we never really connected or talked to each other beyond the surface level conversations or academic context. It wasn't until Fall 2016 that we were finally able to hold far more meaningful conversations after I crashed into her library friend group.
We became friends during Fall 2016 for a variety of reasons. One, we both shared more classes together that academic year than previously. Two, she needed my help for said classes. Three, she had a variety of guy issues and she needed advice. So I served as her main advisor regarding how to deal with her guy problems. The details of this are not necessary and have long since become a thing of the past.
What I like about Caroline is the fact that she's a very loyal and genuine individual. She's someone who strives to live an authentic life to the best of her abilities and surrounds herself with people who will help her live a genuine and honest life. I consider Caroline to be a life long friend in that she's been in my life for about 17 years so far, and we've been friends for at least 5 years. And even if it weren't for the chronological value, it's also the fact that Caroline helps remind me about my boundaries and ensures that I don't go too far in my analysis of other people. She also is more than willing to suggest me more moral alternatives that I normally don't consider whenever I'm making judgments that could impact others in a large and meaningful way.
