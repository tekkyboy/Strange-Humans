The Epic Movement era (Spring 2022 - Present)

This is an ongoing moment in my life. I don't know how long this era of my life will last. All I do know is that it feels different this time.

About Epic Movement Me:

"God has created me the way that I am for a reason. God has sent me on the path I travelled for a reason. I just don't know what that reason is yet."

There is this side of me that most people don't know about me. I'm a Catholic Christian. Have been for at least five years now. Went through the confirmation process during the High School era of my life. I was raised as a Catholic for my childhood. But being a Catholic isn't something that I go about openly expressing for many reasons. After living a social life with many non-Christians, I've decided that it was time for me to return to my roots and embrace the person that I was before I was a teenager. Being in Epic Movement has helped me embrace my Christian roots.

Since I am one of the older members in the organization, I tend to see everyone below my year as significantly younger in terms of both age and life experience. I feel like I act like a dad or a grandfather towards the freshmen especially.

About Epic Movement:

Epic Movement is an organizaiton targeted towards young adult Asian Christians. It is an organization where all Asian Christians can develop a relationship with God at their own rate and form close friendships with those with similar backgrounds to them.

Historically, Epic Movement has been around Cal Poly Pomona since 2006. But like many college organizations, it went on hiatus during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. But unlike many college organizations, Epic Movement persisted online as the memberships were composed of Christians seeking a realtionship with God through others. Therefore, unlike say a college fraternity that benefits from in person social interactions, a college Christian youth group persisted through the power of their faith alone.

Furthermore, if the idea of forming relationships with other young adults who believe in God through an online setting is too much for you to comprehend or understand, then maybe this group isn't for you.

About my Epic Movement era friendships:

As this is a developing era in my life, it's too soon to tell what kind of people my friends are and the quality of relationships I will have with each of them. I do know that some of them are life long friends through what I would call the Will of God alone. The Will of God is something that can't be easily proven, just something easily accepted if you're a Christian. At the very least, all of my friends appear to seek genuine relationships both with God and the people around them.
