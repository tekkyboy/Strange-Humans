Paola Corazon

"I'm just trying to do my best in life, for myself and for my family."

  • Gender: Female
  • Eye Color: Brown
  • Hair Color: Black
  • Hair Length: Long, to chest
  • Zodiac Birth Sign: Taurus
I met Paola while we were pledging for Alpha Phi Omega. We were both in the same car going to Initiation Night's after event at In-N-Out. She noticed that I had the TARDIS as a my lock screen, and we had a conversation about Doctor Who throughout the car ride.
We became friends trying to get to Huntington Beach for a Corgi Beach Day. She was the only one going to the event who offered to drive all the way from El Monte to Huntington Beach. Along the way, we got lost, got stuck in traffic, but we bonded trying to find our way to the event itself. What furthered our friendship were our common interest in Doctor Who and growing up Catholic in a non-religious high school.
Paola's my Alpha Phi Omega twin. She is my friend for life because of that. But even if we weren't Alpha Phi Omega twins, we would be friends because of our shared interest in Doctor Who. And even if it wasn't for Doctor Who, we would be friends because we both have had the same experience growing up and not feeling like we belonged to any one group. It was that shared pain of growing up with extreme social anxiety about the future and our social lives, which makes me trust her a lot.
And I honestly consider Paola to be a better person than me in so many ways. Where I saught violence, she saught peace. She was always there to keep me in check, to help remind me that there are other ways to solve problems. That there are other ways to live life rather than feeling angry against the rest of the world. She was there to give me hope when it seemed like almost everyone else just wouldn't get it. So I appreciate her a lot because of her being a part of my heart.
